How We Protect You

Spring Bank uses multiple layers of security to keep your accounts and personal information safe:

  • 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption — all data transmitted between you and Spring Bank is encrypted
  • Multi-factor authentication — verify your identity with a second factor on every login
  • Fraud monitoring — AI-powered real-time detection flags unusual activity instantly
  • Biometric login — use fingerprint or face ID in our mobile app
  • Session timeouts — automatic logout after periods of inactivity
  • Card controls — lock/unlock your card instantly in the Spring app

Zero Liability Protection

Spring Bank's Zero Liability Policy means you're not responsible for unauthorized transactions on your Spring debit or credit card — as long as you report them promptly. We'll investigate and resolve disputes within 10 business days.

FDIC Insurance

Your deposits are insured by the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (FDIC) up to $250,000 per depositor, per account ownership category. This is a federal guarantee — your money is protected even if Spring Bank were to fail.

How to Stay Safe

  • Never share your password, PIN, or full Social Security number via email or text
  • Spring Bank will never ask for this information via unsolicited contact
  • Use a unique, strong password for your Spring account
  • Enable two-factor authentication in your account settings
  • Review your account statements regularly and report anything suspicious immediately
  • Use only the official Spring Bank app from the App Store or Google Play

Report a Security Concern

If you suspect fraud or unauthorized activity: Call 1-800-SPRING-1 (24/7) immediately. You can also report phishing emails to phishing@springbank.com.
