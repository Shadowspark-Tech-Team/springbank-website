## ⚡ Deploy in 3 Steps

### Step 1: Go to Netlify
👉 Visit: **https://app.netlify.com**
- Click **"Sign in"** or **"Sign up"**
- Choose **"Continue with GitHub"**
- Authorize Netlify

### Step 2: Add New Site
- Click **"Add new site"** → **"Import an existing project"**
- Choose **"Deploy with GitHub"**
- Find and select: **`Shadow7user/springbank-website`**
- Select branch: **`copilot/create-banking-demo-website`**

### Step 3: Deploy
- **Build command**: Leave empty (static site)
- **Publish directory**: Leave as `.` (root)
- Click **"Deploy site"**

⏱️ **Wait 2-3 minutes**

✅ **DONE! Your site is LIVE!**

---

## 🌐 Your Live URL

Netlify will give you:
```
https://[random-name].netlify.app
```

**Example**: `https://wonderful-spring-123abc.netlify.app`

You can change this to a custom name in Site settings!
