Frequently Asked Questions

Account Access

Visit the sign-in page and click "Forgot username/password?" Follow the on-screen steps to verify your identity and reset your credentials. If you're locked out, call us at 1-800-SPRING-1 (24/7).

Log in to your account, go to Settings → Security → Two-Step Verification. You can receive verification codes via text message, authenticator app, or email. We strongly recommend enabling 2FA for all accounts.

For your protection, Spring Bank automatically locks accounts after multiple failed sign-in attempts. To unlock your account, click "Forgot username/password?" on the sign-in page, or call 1-800-SPRING-1.

Payments & Transfers

Zelle® is built into the Spring Bank mobile app and online banking. Go to Pay & Transfer → Send money with Zelle®, then enter the recipient's email or US mobile number. Transfers between enrolled users are typically instant.

Domestic wire transfers are $35 per outgoing transfer. International wires are $40. Online wire transfers via Spring Online Banking are typically processed same day if submitted before 4 PM ET on business days. Limits vary by account type.

Cards & Accounts

Immediately lock or cancel your card in the Spring Bank app under Card Services, or call 1-800-SPRING-1. You're protected by Zero Liability — you won't be charged for unauthorized transactions reported promptly. A replacement card arrives in 3–5 business days (or 1–2 days expedited).

Log in to Spring Online Banking, find the transaction, and select "Dispute this transaction." You can also call 1-800-SPRING-1. Disputes are typically resolved within 10 business days. You may receive a provisional credit while we investigate.

Open a new Spring Total Checking® account and set up qualifying direct deposits totaling $500 or more within the first 90 days. The $300 bonus is deposited into your account within 15 business days after the qualifying period ends. New customers only. Terms apply.

Security & Fraud

Do not click any links or provide personal information. Forward the email to phishing@springbank.com and then delete it. Spring Bank will never ask for your full Social Security number, account password, or card PIN via email or text.

Call 1-800-SPRING-1 immediately. Our Fraud team is available 24/7. You can also freeze your account instantly in the Spring Bank app. Under Zero Liability, you won't be held responsible for unauthorized charges reported promptly.

Still need help?

Our team is available 24/7 by phone, and Mon–Fri 8am–8pm ET by chat.
