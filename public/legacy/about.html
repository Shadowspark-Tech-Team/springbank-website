Our Story

Banking built for
real people.

Spring Bank was founded in 2010 with a single conviction: banking should work for people, not the other way around. Today we serve more than 5 million customers across the United States — and we're just getting started.

Our Mission

Make banking simpler, fairer, and more human

We believe everyone deserves a bank they can trust — one that gives them the tools to grow, protect, and make the most of their money without the jargon, hidden fees, or friction that has defined banking for too long.

Spring was built from the ground up around the customer. Our technology is best-in-class, our fees are transparent, and our people are trained to listen first and sell second.

What we stand for

Six principles that guide every decision we make — from product design to customer service.

Trust & Integrity

We keep our word. No hidden fees, no surprise rate hikes, no fine print designed to trick you.

People First

Our customers and employees are our greatest asset. We invest in both, always.

Innovation

We move fast, ship often, and build technology that solves real problems — not technology for its own sake.

Inclusion

Banking is for everyone. We build products that are accessible, equitable, and useful across every income level.

Sustainability

Transparency

Our history

From a two-person startup to a national bank — here's how we got here.

2010
Founded in New York City
Spring Bank opens its first branch in Manhattan with 12 employees and a bold mission: make banking work for people, not the other way around.
2012
First 100,000 customers
Word spreads about our no-fee checking accounts and human customer service. We reach 100K customers 18 months ahead of projections.
2015
Spring Business Banking launches
We extend our philosophy to small businesses, offering checking accounts with no minimum balance and SBA-preferred lending.
2018
Spring Mobile App — 4.9★ on App Store
Our redesigned app wins "Best Banking App" from NerdWallet and reaches 2 million active users within 6 months.
2021
Spring Sapphire Preferred® card launches
Our flagship rewards card earns industry acclaim for its earning rates and travel protections.
2024
5 million customers & $42B AUM
Spring Bank becomes a nationally chartered bank with branches in 34 states and 4,800+ ATMs. We remain founder-led and customer-obsessed.
