# ⚡ Vercel Quickstart - Deploy in 3 Minutes

## 🚀 Fast Deploy (3 Steps)

### Step 1: Go to Vercel
👉 **https://vercel.com**
- Click **"Continue with GitHub"**
- Authorize access

### Step 2: Import Project
- Click **"Add New Project"**
- Select: **`Shadow7user/springbank-website`**
- Branch: **`copilot/create-banking-demo-website`**
- Click **"Import"**

### Step 3: Deploy
- Leave all settings as default
- Click **"Deploy"**
- ⏱️ Wait 2-3 minutes
- ✅ **DONE!**
